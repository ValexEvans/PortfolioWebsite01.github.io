Compound Planetary Reducer:
 I used Fusion 360 to design a hand and reducer for a robotic prosthetic hand company. The goal of the project was to increase the grip strength of the hand. The reducer I designed was a compound planetary reducer, consisting of two planetary reducers with a 1:3 and 1:4 gear ratio, respectively, for a total ratio of 1:12. The hand and reducer were 3D printed in PLA and assembled.
Myoelectric Robotic Hand
 I used Autodesk Inventor to design and put together a 3D printed hand. I also programmed a C++-based microcontroller and paired it with an EMG sensor, which allowed me to control the hand with muscle movements.
